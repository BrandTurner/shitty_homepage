Who am I?

I am a freelancer, a code warrior searching for a righteous tech company.

Since my early years, I've always been interested in the confluence of science, technology, and the arts. Inspired by Renaissance and Enlighenment era polymaths, I've always aspired to become a jack of all trades and a master of many. In the past, I have worked as a director on a poltical campaign,contributed to the annotation of the Tribolium castaneum genome, and helped bring genetic testing to the masses

As I evolved into a software engineer, I have helped several companies build great products that interface with hundreds of thousands of users worldwide and I have been exposed to a multiple technology stacks such as Java, PHP, Python and Javascript. My goal is to create business value using my proven expertise in agile, test-driven software development. As a consummate team player, I emphasize inter and intra-team communication

If you can't tell, I am slightly obsessed with Mad Max: Fury Road
